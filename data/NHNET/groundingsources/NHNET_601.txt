When news broke that reality television star Kristin Cavallari was divorcing her husband, the internet let out an audible gasp. For three seasons of the hit reality series Very Cavallari, the TV personality and her husband Jay Cutler were the picture of marital bliss. However, in April 2020, the mother-of-three announced she was separating from Cutler after 10 years together.
In a somber Instagram post, the star wrote, "With great sadness, after 10 years together we have come to a loving conclusion to get a divorce. We have nothing but love and respect for one another and are deeply grateful for the years shared, memories made, and the children we are so proud of."
Cavallari also added, "This is just the situation of two people growing apart. We ask everyone to respect our privacy as we navigate this difficult time within our family." Although she has been open about the separation being "the hardest decision" she's ever made, the fashion designer may be moving on already.
