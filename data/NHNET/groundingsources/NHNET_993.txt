After six days of chasing him,gangster, who is the main accused in thebloodbath that left eight policemen dead, has been arrested on Thursday.Eight policemen, including a deputy superintendent of police, were ambushed in Bikru village in the Chaubeypur area of Kanpur, where they had gone to arrest Vikas Dubey and fell to bullets fired from rooftops last Friday.According to reports, Dubey who gave the police a slip just a day earlier from Faridabad, was arrested from Ujjain. He was also carrying a reward of Rs 5 lakh on his head.The arrest comes within hours after two of his aides were gunned down in separate encounters.While Kartikeya alias Prabhat was killed in Kanpur when he tried to flee from police custody, another aide of Vikas Dubey, Praveen alias Bauwa Dubey, was shot dead in an encounter in Etawah, police claimed.On Wednesday, another aide Amar Dubey was also killed in the encounter.The police got a tip-off that Praveen and three others had stolen a car near Mahewa in the Bakewar area of Etawah, the ADG said.Five persons have so far been killed in separate police encounters since the Kanpur ambush.Earlier, Amar Dubey was killed in Hamirpur, while Prem Prakash Pandey and Atul Dubey were gunned down in an encounter in Kanpur on July 3.
