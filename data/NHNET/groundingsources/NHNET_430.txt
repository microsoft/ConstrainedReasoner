The Republican party has an extremist problem, and the face of that problem is Marjorie Taylor Greene. The Georgia congresswoman was caught on tape in 2019 harassing Parkland shooting survivor, David Hogg. Hogg sits down with MSNBC’s host of “American Voices” Alicia Menendez to discuss the incident, as well as the larger threat posed by Greene and her cohorts in the post-riot GOP.
