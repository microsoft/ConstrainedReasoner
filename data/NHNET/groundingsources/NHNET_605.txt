There won’t be any trick-or-treating with Mickey Mouse this year.
The 2020 Mickey’s Not-So-Scary Halloween Party is canceled.
Walt Disney World says they don’t want to present the stage shows, fireworks, and parade with the coronavirus still posing a threat.
The company has already canceled several evening events at other parks. No decision yet on whether Mickey’s Very Merry Christmas Party will also be canceled.
