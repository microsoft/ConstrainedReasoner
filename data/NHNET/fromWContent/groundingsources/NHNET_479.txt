Petrol, Diesel Prices Today: Petrol prices were hiked by 25 paise to touch an all-time high in the national capital on Saturday, ie January 23. In the national capital, the price of petrol was increased by 25 paise from Rs 85.45 per litre to ₹ 85.70 and diesel was hiked by an equal amount from ₹ 75.63 per litre to ₹ 75.88 per litre, according to Indian Oil Corporation, the country's largest fuel retailer.
