CANBERRA, June 18 (Xinhua) -- Australia's unemployment rate has risen to 7.1 percent amid the coronavirus pandemic. According to the latest labor force data published by the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS) on Thursday, 227,700 jobs were lost in May, bringing the unemployment rate from 6.4 percent in April to 7.1 percent in May.
