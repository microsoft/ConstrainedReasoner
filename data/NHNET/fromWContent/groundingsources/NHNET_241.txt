Realme has officially released the Realme GT 5G, its first performance flagship of 2021. The Realme GT 5G is based on the concept of GT sports cars, which were designed for high speed and long-distance driving. The Realme GT 5G is equipped with the latest generation of processing platform - Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 and it comes with a 5nm advanced process and Cortex-X1 high-performance core.
