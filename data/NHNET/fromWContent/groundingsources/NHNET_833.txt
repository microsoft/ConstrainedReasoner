A group of fishermen off the coast of Sydney have reeled in a whopping 394kg tiger shark. The fishermen took more than 45 minutes trying to pull in the huge tiger shark, before they headed back to Port Hacking in Sydney's Sutherland Shire with their catch for it to be weighed. The massive shark could barely be contained on the boat, with images showing one of the fishermen holding onto the shark's fin to stop it tipping back ...
