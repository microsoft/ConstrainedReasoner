Brisbane (Australia) - A surfer has died after being attacked by a three-metre shark at a popular beach off Australia's east coast, police said Sunday. The man was surfing at Kingscliff, about 100 kilometres (62 miles) south of Brisbane, on Sunday morning when the shark mauled his leg. “Police have been told several board-riders came to his assistance and fought the shark off before the injured man could be helped to shore,” police said ...
