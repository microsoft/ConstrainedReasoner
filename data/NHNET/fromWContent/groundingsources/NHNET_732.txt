Google's Pixel 4a was late to the game this year. It was supposed to launch at I/O back in May, and then I/O was canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. It finally arrived earlier this month. The reception was mixed, mainly because Google removed any choice from its new device. Indeed, there's no XL model, it only comes in black, and there's just one storage configuration. There are no variants of the Pixel 4a at all ...
