(Reuters) - Coty Inc COTY.N is set to announce that Peter Harf, its current chairman, will become the cosmetics company's new chief executive officer, replacing Pierre Laubies, the Wall Street Journal reported on Sunday, citing people familiar with the matter. Harf will assume the CEO role as soon as Monday, the Journal reported, making him Coty's fourth chief executive in less than four years. Coty declined to comment on the report ...
