PARIS (Reuters) - French President Emmanuel Macron will deliver a televised address to the nation on Wednesday evening, his office said, as a fast-spreading third wave of COVID-19 infections threatens to over-run hospitals. The Elysee Palace did not say whether Macron would announce further restrictions to counter the virus as the country's death toll nears 100,000.
