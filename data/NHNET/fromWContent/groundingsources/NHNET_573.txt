The drama film “Minari” about a Korean-American immigrant family was named the best foreign language film at the US Golden Globe Awards on Monday (Korean time). In a ceremony held virtually, “Minari” won the best motion picture in foreign language, beating “Another Round” from Denmark, “La Llorona” from Guatemala, “The Life Ahead” from Italy and “Two of Us” from the France.
