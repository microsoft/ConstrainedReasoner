DETROIT -- Toyota says it has recalled roughly 752,000 Prius models due to a fail-safe issue that may cause the engine to lose power and stall. According to the Associated Press, the recall applies to certain 2013 to 2015 Prius and 2014 to 2017 Prius v hybrids. Approximately 267,000 vehicles in the US will be impacted by the recall.
