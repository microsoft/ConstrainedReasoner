Bob Dylan's entire catalog of songs dating back 60 years is being acquired by Universal Music Publishing Group. The catalog contains 600 song copyrights including “Blowin' In The Wind,” “The Times They Are a-Changin',” “Knockin' On Heaven's Door,” and “Tangled Up In Blue."
