Donald Trump paid a visit to Trump International Golf Club in West Palm Beach, Fla. on Dec. 27, representing the 304th time the 45th President has visited one of his 17 golf clubs (and, for most of them, presumably played some golf) since becoming President on Jan. 20, 2017.
