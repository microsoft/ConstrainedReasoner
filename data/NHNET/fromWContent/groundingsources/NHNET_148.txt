The second wave of COVID-19 in India may last for up to 100 days. This was stated in a report published by the research team of the State Bank of India (SBI). “The entire duration of second wave might last up to 100 days counted from 15 February,” said a report from SBI's research team. The 100-day duration has been counted from February 15. The report has been authored by SBI's chief economic adviser – Soumya Kanti Ghosh ...
