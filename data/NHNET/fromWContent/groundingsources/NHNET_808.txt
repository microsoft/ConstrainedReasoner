The capture by two Florida men of an 18-foot, 9-inch Burmese python has set a length record for state programs designed to control the spread of the invasive species. Ryan Ausburn and Kevin Pavlidis caught the 104-pound python on Oct. 2 in the Everglades, as members of the Python Action Team and South Florida Water Management District's Python Elimination Program.
