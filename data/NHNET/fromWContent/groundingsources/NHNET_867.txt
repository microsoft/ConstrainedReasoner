Forecasters have declared a hurricane warning for parts of the Florida coast as Hurricane Isaias drenched the Bahamas on a track for the US east coast. Isaias had maximum sustained winds of 130km/h late on Friday and was expected to strengthen through early Saturday, the US National Hurricane Center in Miami said.
