The death toll due to Coronavirus rose to 779 and the number of cases climbed to 24,942 in the country on Saturday, according to the Union Health Ministry. The number of active COVID-19 cases stood at 18,668 while 5,062 people were cured and discharged, and one patient has migrated, the ministry said.
