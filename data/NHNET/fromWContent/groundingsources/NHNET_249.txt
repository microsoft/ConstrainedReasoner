Dropping in September, Tickets To My Downfall saw Machine Gun Kelly put rap on the back burner this time round, instead deciding to produce his own 'love letter' to pop punk. The album is a tight 15-track package of guitar-driven power vibes, featuring the likes of Halsey, Blackbear, not to mention Yungblud and The Used's Bert McCracken for good measure.
