NASA is keeping a close eye on a massive asteroid leading up to the weekend, according to a report by the UK's Daily Times. The asteroid is estimated to be between 820 feet and 1,870 feet, making it potentially taller than the Empire State Building at 1,453 feet, and it's traveling at a speed of 11,200 miles per hour.
