German Chancellor Angela Merkel has reversed a planned strict Easter shutdown, the chief of her CDU party said, amid massive criticism of the government's pandemic management. Ms Merkel and the leaders of Germany's 16 states had agreed at marathon talks on Monday that almost all shops were to be closed from 1 April to 5 April, with only grocers allowed to open on Saturday 3 April.
