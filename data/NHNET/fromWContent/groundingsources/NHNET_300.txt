NEW YORK: Gaming platform Roblox – which has skyrocketed in popularity among kids and teens during the coronavirus pandemic – will make its Wall Street debut Wednesday as a direct listing. The company's shares will be listed under the symbol RBLX on the New York Stock Exchange.
