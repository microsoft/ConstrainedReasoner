Near-Earth asteroids, or NEAs, pass by our home planet all the time. But an SUV-size asteroid set the record this past weekend for coming closer to Earth than any other known NEA: It passed 1,830 miles (2,950 kilometres) above the southern Indian Ocean on Sunday, August 16, at 12:08 am EDT (Saturday, August 15, at 9:08 pm PDT).
