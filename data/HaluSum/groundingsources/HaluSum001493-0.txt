Ryuichi Kiyonari bettered the Donington Park lap record to take put his Buildbase BMW on to pole start for the Easter Monday opening round of the MCE British Superbike Championship. The Japanese rider powered in a scorching time of one minute 29.455 seconds. In doing so, he headed off the close attentions of James Ellison on the JG Speedfit Kawasaki by just 0.029secs.
