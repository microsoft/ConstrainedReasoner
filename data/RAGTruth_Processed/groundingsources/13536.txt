'Young and the Restless' Spoilers: Adam And Sally Hit The Sheets, Nick Loses Her Forever?
The Young and the Restless (Y&R) spoilers for the week of October 23 tease that Adam Newman (Mark Grossman) and Sally Spectra (Courtney Hope) will move toward reuniting. Soap Opera Digest reports that Sally is struggling with her unresolved feelings for Adam and her painful breakup with Nick Newman (Joshua Morrow).
Sally and Nick discuss her business venture. Soaps Spoilers reports that Sally asks Nick if he plans to pull the funding for her business now that they broke up. Nick tells her that he considered it but decided not to do that to her. He believes in her talent and wants her to succeed.
Nick urges Sally to follow her heart and pick the man who will make her happy. If she doesn't, she will never feel content. He hopes that is him, but Nick is prepared for Sally to pick his brother if that's what she wants.
The Young and the Restless spoilers say that Sally spots Adam having a drink at the GCAC. Adam can tell that Sally is upset about something and asks her to open up to him. She tells him that it was a mistake talking to him. She heads upstairs to her suite. Adam follows her.
In her suite, Adam confesses he's really worried about his father. They embrace, and when they pull apart, they look into each other's eyes and hit the sheets. Adam asks Sally if she wants to stop, to which she replies no, she wants to be with him at that moment.
Even though they reconnected, Sally isn't sure if she wants to be with him. She's still conflicted between Nick and Adam. She cares deeply about both men. It seems like an impossible task to choose one of them.
The Young and the Restless spoilers say that Nick will discover Sally and Adam were together. He decides breaking up with Sally is the best move to protect his heart from getting shattered by Sally's complicated feelings for Adam.
