The Peshawar Zalmi is a franchise cricket team that represents Peshawar in the Pakistan Super League. The team was captained by Shahid Afridi, and they stand on first position after winning six matches from their eight matches in the PSL 2016.Sahibzada Mohammad Shahid Khan Afridi (Urdu: ‎ ; Pashto: شاهد اپریدی‎ ; born 1980), popularly as Shahid "Boom Boom" Afridi, is a former Pakistani cricketer and former captain of the Pakistan national cricket team.
