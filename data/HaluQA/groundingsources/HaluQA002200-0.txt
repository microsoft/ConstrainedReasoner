"I Would Die 4 U" is a song by Prince and The Revolution, and the fourth single in the US from their 1984 album, "Purple Rain".Purple Rain is the sixth studio album by American recording artist Prince, the first to feature his band The Revolution, and is the soundtrack to the 1984 film of the same name. To date, it has sold over 25 million copies worldwide, making it the third-best-selling soundtrack album of all time.
