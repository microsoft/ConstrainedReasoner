Deep Pal (born April 16, 1953) is an Indian cinematographer. His later work included "Bandit Queen" directed by Shekhar Kapur and "Dil Se.Dil Se (translation: "From the Heart)" is a 1998 Indian romantic thriller film in the backdrop of insurgency in Northeast India, written and directed by Mani Ratnam, and produced by Mani Ratnam, Ram Gopal Varma, and Shekhar Kapur.
