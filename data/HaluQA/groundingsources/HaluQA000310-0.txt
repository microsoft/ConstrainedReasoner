Argentinean label Dias De Garage released the first-ever tribute to the rock legend Kyuss in July 2004. Titled "Listen Without Distraction", the collection was named after the instructions Kyuss used to give music fans on their albums.Kyuss ( ) was an American rock band, formed in Palm Desert, California, in 1987 by Josh Homme (guitar), John Garcia (vocals), Brant Bjork (drums) and Chris Cockrell (bass).
