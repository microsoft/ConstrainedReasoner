King (born November 15, 1972) is an American director and writer.Ivan Tors (born Iván Törzs; June 12, 1916  in Budapest, Hungary– June 4, 1983 in the Brazilian state of Mato Grosso) was a Hungarian playwright, film director, screenwriter, and film and television producer with an emphasis on non-violent but exciting science fiction, underwater sequences, and stories involving animals.
