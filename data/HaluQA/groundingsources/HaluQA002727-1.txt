Aaron Waisglass, better known by his stage name Grandtheft, is a Canadian DJ and musician based in Toronto. He gained recognition as a trap artist, for remixing songs like “Summer“, “Sweet Nothing“ and “Bitch Better Have My Money” and having songs charted around the world."Bitch Better Have My Money" is a song recorded by Barbadian singer Rihanna. The song was digitally released on March 26, 2015, through the iTunes Store.
