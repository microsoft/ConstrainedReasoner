Lucy Elizabeth Fry (born 13 March 1992) is an Australian actress. Fry was also cast in Hulu's eight part miniseries "11.22.63" as Marina Oswald, wife of Lee Harvey Oswald, and played the lead in the 2016 Australian horror television series "Wolf Creek".Wolf Creek is a 2016 Australian horror web television series which screens on Stan. It is a spin-off of the movies "Wolf Creek" and "Wolf Creek 2" and follows Eve, a 19-year-old American tourist, who is targeted by crazed serial killer Mick Taylor, survives his attack and embarks on a mission of revenge.
