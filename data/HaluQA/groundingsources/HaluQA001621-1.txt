"Fifteen" is a country pop song performed by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. "Fifteen" was released on August 30, 2009 by Big Machine Records, as the fourth single from Swift's second studio album, "Fearless" (2008).Fearless is the second studio album by the American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. As with her first album, "Taylor Swift", Swift wrote or co-wrote all thirteen tracks on "Fearless". Swift also made her debut as a record producer, co-producing all songs on the album with Nathan Chapman.
