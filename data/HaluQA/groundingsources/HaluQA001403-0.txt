Sonic Highways is a 2014 American documentary miniseries directed by Dave Grohl and written by Mark Monroe. Each of the eight episodes is presented as an exploration of the musical history of a different American city through a series of interviews by Grohl. He is the former drummer of the grunge band Nirvana and the frontman and founder of the rock band Foo Fighters, of which he is the lead vocalist, rhythm and lead guitarist, and primary songwriter.
