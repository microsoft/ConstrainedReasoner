Robin Weaver is an English actress well known for playing the role of recurring character Pamela Cooper in the E4 sitcom "The Inbetweeners" and its feature-length films, "The Inbetweeners Movie" and "The Inbetweeners 2".The Inbetweeners 2 is a 2014 British comedy film and sequel to "The Inbetweeners Movie" (2011), which is based on the E4 sitcom "The Inbetweeners".
