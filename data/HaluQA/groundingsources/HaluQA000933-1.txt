"Good Life" is a song recorded by American rapper G-Eazy and American singer Kehlani, taken from the soundtrack of the 2017 action film "The Fate of the Furious" (2017).The Fate of the Furious (alternatively known as Fast & Furious 8 and Fast 8, and often stylized as F8) is a 2017 American action film directed by F. Gary Gray and written by Chris Morgan.
