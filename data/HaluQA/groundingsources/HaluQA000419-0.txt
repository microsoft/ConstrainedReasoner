The DFB Sports Court (German: DFB-Sportgericht) is a regulatory body in the German Football Association (German: "Deutscher Fussball-Bund" , DFB) and may adopt different sanctions on clubs and players. The 21 state associations of the DFB have a combined number of more than 25,000 clubs with more than 6.8 million members, making the DFB the single largest sports federation in the world.
