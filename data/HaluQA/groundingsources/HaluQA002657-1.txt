 The sonata was first performed on 30 December 1944 in Moscow by Emil Gilels.Emil Grigoryevich Gilels (sometimes transliterated "Hilels"; , Russian: Эми́ль Григо́рьевич Ги́лельс , "Emiľ Grigorievič Gileľs "; 19 October 1916 – 14 October 1985) was a Soviet pianist, widely regarded as one of the greatest pianists of the 20th century.
