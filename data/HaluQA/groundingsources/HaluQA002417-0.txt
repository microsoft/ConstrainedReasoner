The 2004–05 NBA season was the Bucks' 37th season in the National Basketball Association. During the offseason, the Bucks signed free agents Mike James and second-year guard Mo Williams, while acquiring Zaza Pachulia from the expansion Charlotte Bobcats.Zaza Pachulia (Georgian: ზაზა ფაჩულია ; February 10, 1984) is a Georgian professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA).
