 Following a lengthy hiatus, they returned, releasing second album "Everything You've Come to Expect" in 2016.The Classic Crime is an American rock band from Seattle, Washington formed in 2004. They have released three albums and an EP on Tooth & Nail Records, two of which, "The Silver Cord" (2008) and "Vagabonds" (2010), charted in the "Billboard" 200. In July 2011, the band left Tooth & Nail to produce a fourth album with the help of fan donations via Kickstarter, called "Phoenix" (2012). In April 2016, The Classic Crime created a Kickstarter to raise funds for a fifth studio album.
