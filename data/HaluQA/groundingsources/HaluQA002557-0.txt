 Laustsen is best known for his work on the films "Silent Hill", "The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen", and "Crimson Peak," where the visual side of "The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen" was hailed as a "visual treat" in Variety. It was directed by Stephen Norrington and starred Sean Connery, Naseeruddin Shah, Peta Wilson, Tony Curran, Stuart Townsend, Shane West, Jason Flemyng, and Richard Roxburgh.
