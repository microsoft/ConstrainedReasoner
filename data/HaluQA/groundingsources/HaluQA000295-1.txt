Akhilendra Mishra is an Indian film and television character actor best known for his role as Kroor Singh in the 1990s Doordarshan fantasy television series "Chandrakanta". His other notable works include the character of Mirchi Seth in the 1999 critically acclaimed film "Sarfarosh".Lagaan (English: "Taxation" ; also called "Lagaan: Once Upon a Time in India") is a 2001 Indian epic sports-drama film written and directed by Ashutosh Gowariker.
