Evan Mecham ( ; May 12, 1924 – February 21, 2008) was the 17th Governor of Arizona. Periodic runs for political office earned him a reputation as a perennial candidate along with the nickname of "The Harold Stassen of Arizona" before he was elected governor, under the Republican banner.Harold Edward Stassen (April 13, 1907 – March 4, 2001) was the 25th Governor of Minnesota.
