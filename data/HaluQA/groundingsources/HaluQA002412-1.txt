Run Fatboy Run is a 2007 British-American comedy film directed by David Schwimmer, written by Michael Ian Black and Simon Pegg, and starring Pegg, Dylan Moran, Thandie Newton, Harish Patel, India de Beaufort, and Hank Azaria.David Lawrence Schwimmer (born November 2, 1966) is an American actor, director, and producer. In 1988, he graduated from Northwestern University with a Bachelor of Arts in theater and speech.
