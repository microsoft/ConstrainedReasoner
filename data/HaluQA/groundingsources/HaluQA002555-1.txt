KLCC District Cooling is a building that is used to provide district cooling for the KLCC vicinity. It is a 30,000 ton gas-powered turbine-driven chilled water unit providing cool air not only to Petronas Twin Towers and Suria KLCC, but also to Maxis Tower, Menara Exxon Mobil, Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre, and Mandarin Oriental Kuala Lumpur as well as As Syakirin Mosque.Suria KLCC is a shopping centre located in central Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
