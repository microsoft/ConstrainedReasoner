Krantiveer(English:"Brave Revolutionary") is a 1994 Indian Hindi-language action crime film directed by Mehul Kumar. The film stars Nana Patekar, Dimple Kapadia, Atul Agnihotri, Mamta Kulkarni, Danny Denzongpa and Paresh Rawal in lead roles."Nana" Patekar, born 1 January 1951) is an Indian actor, writer and filmmaker, mainly working in Hindi and Marathi films.
