The 2017 Supercheap Auto Bathurst 1000 will be a motor racing event for Supercars, held on the weekend of 5 to 8 October 2017. It is situated on a hill with the dual official names of Mount Panorama and Wahluu and is best known as the home of the Bathurst 1000 motor race held each October, and the Bathurst 12 Hour event held each February.
