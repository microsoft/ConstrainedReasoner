Jeanette Miller is an American character actress who has appeared in theatre, film and television since the 1940s She is perhaps best known for her recurring role as Aunt Edie on the ABC comedy series "The Middle", which she played from 2009 until early 2015, when her character was said to have died from old age. In film, she appeared in "Cold Heaven", "The Truman Show", "Norbit", "Four Christmases" and "Legion".Four Christmases (Four Holidays in Australia and New Zealand, Anywhere But Home in the Netherlands, Norway, United Arab Emirates and in South Africa) is a Christmas-themed romantic comedy film about a couple visiting all four of their divorced parents' homes on Christmas Day. The film is produced by Spyglass Entertainment released by New Line Cinema on November 26, 2008, the day before Thanksgiving, and distributed by Warner Bros.
