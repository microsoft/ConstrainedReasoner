The Guilty Mother (French: "La Mère coupable" ) subtitled "The Other Tartuffe" is the third play of the "Figaro" trilogy by Pierre Beaumarchais; its predecessors were "The Barber of Seville" and "The Marriage of Figaro".Tartuffe, or The Impostor, or The Hypocrite ( ; French: "Tartuffe, ou l'Imposteur" , ] ), first performed in 1664, is one of the most famous theatrical comedies by Molière.
