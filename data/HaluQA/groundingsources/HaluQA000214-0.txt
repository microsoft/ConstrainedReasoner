Anti-Semitism in the 21st Century: The Resurgence is a documentary film that first aired on PBS on January 8, 2007. Directed, produced, and written by Andrew Goldberg, this documentary, hosted by Judy Woodruff, examines the roots of modern antisemitism and why it flourishes today.Judith "Judy" Woodruff (born November 20, 1946) is anchor of PBS NewsHour. She is also a journalist and writer.
