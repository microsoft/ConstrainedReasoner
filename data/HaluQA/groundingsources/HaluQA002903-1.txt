The Wars series, also known as Famicom Wars (ファミコンウォーズ , Famikon Wōzu ) in Japan, Advance Wars in the West, and occasionally Nintendo Wars as a whole, is a series of military turn-based tactics video games, usually developed by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo.Fire Emblem is a tactical Role-playing video game franchise developed by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo. First produced and published for the Family Computer (Famicom), the series consists of twelve original games, three remakes, and two spin-off, that are playable across multiple game systems.
