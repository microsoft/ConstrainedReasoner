Shayaa Bin Abraham-Joseph (born October 22, 1992), better known by his stage name 21 Savage, is an American rapper from Atlanta, Georgia. He was best known for his mixtapes, "The Slaughter Tape" and "Slaughter King" and his extended plays "Free Guwop" and "Savage Mode" with Metro Boomin before the release of his debut album, "ISSA" and its lead single, "Bank Account".Issa Album is the debut studio album by American rapper 21 Savage. The album was released on July 7, 2017 by Slaughter Gang and Epic Records.
