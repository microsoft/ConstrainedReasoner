Sarah Solemani is an award winning English actress, writer and activist, best known for starring in the BAFTA winning sitcom "Him & Her" , playing Renee Zellweger's best friend 'Miranda' in Working Title's "Bridget Jones's Baby", for which she was nominated for an Evening Standard Best Actress Award, and for her role as Rosie Gulliver in "Bad Education".Him & Her is a British television sitcom about a lazy twenty-something couple: Steve and Becky, who live in Walthamstow, London. It was first broadcast in the United Kingdom on BBC Three on 6 September 2010.
