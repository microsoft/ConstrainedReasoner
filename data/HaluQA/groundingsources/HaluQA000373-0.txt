Avianca S.A. (acronym in Spanish for "Aerovías del Continente Americano S.A. "), Airways of the American Continent, is a Colombian airline that has been the national airline and flag carrier of Colombia since 5 December 1919, when it was initially registered under the name SCADTA. It is wholly owned by Synergy Group S.A., a South American holding company established by Germán Efromovich and specializing in air transport. The group is headquartered in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
