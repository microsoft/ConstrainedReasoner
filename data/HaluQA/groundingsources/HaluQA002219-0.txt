Monfragüe (Spanish: "Parque Nacional Monfragüe") is a Spanish national park noted for its bird-life.Doñana National Park is a natural reserve in Andalusia, southern Spain, in the provinces of Huelva (most of its territory) and Seville. It was established as a nature reserve in 1969 when the World Wildlife Fund joined with the Spanish government and purchased a section of marshes to protect it.
