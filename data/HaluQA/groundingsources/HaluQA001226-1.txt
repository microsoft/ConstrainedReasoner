The skogsrå , (Forest Rå), "Skogsfrun" (The Mistress of the Forest), "Skogssnuvan", "Skogsnymfen" (Forest Nymph), "Råndan" (the Rå) or "huldra", was a mythical creature of the forest, or Rå, in Swedish folklore.A hulder is a seductive forest creature found in Scandinavian folklore. She is known as the skogsrå "forest spirit" or Tallemaja "pine tree Mary" in Swedish folklore, and ulda in Sámi folklore. Her name suggests that she is originally the same being as the völva divine figure Huld and the German Holda.
