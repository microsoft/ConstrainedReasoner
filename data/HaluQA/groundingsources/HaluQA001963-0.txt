The Moscow Hemp Fest (sometimes Moscow Hempfest) is an annual cannabis event held in Moscow, Idaho, in the United States. It began in 1996 for the University of Idaho's Mom's Weekend.The University of Idaho (officially abbreviated UI, locally referred to as "the U of I") is the U.S. state of Idaho's oldest public university, located in the city of Moscow in Latah County in the northern portion of the state.
