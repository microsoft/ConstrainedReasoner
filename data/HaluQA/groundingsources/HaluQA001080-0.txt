The Emperor: Owner of the Mask () is a South Korean television series starring Yoo Seung-ho, Kim So-hyun, Kim Myung-soo, Yoon So-hee, Heo Joon-ho and Park Chul-min. She began her career as a child actress in 2006 and initially gained public attention for playing a villainous young queen-to-be in "Moon Embracing the Sun" (2012) and a girl who falls into tragedy in "Missing You" (2013).
