Yoon Sang-hyun (born September 21, 1973) is a South Korean actor and singer. He is best known for his roles in "Queen of Housewives" (2009), "My Fair Lady" (2009), "Secret Garden" (2011) and "I Can Hear Your Voice" (2013).I Can Hear Your Voice () is a 2013 South Korean television series starring Lee Bo-young, Lee Jong-suk, Yoon Sang-hyun and Lee Da-hee. It aired on SBS from June 5 to August 1, 2013, on Wednesdays and Thursdays at 21:55 for 18 episodes.
