The Desire to Live (Spanish: "El Deseo de vivir" ) is a 1973 Argentine romantic musical film directed by Julio Saraceni and starring Sandro and Elena Sedova.Roberto Sánchez-Ocampo (August 19, 1945 – January 4, 2010), better known by his artist names Sandro/Sandro de América ("Sandro of America"), "Gitano" (gypsy), and the Argentine Elvis, was a notable Argentine singer and actor.
