Akbari Asghari is a Pakistani drama based on Mirat-ul-Uroos. In 2011, the story of Mirat-ul-Uroos was adapted into a comedy television drama airing on Hum TV by the name of Akbari Asghari, in which the lead role of Akbari has been played by Sanam Baloch and Asghari by Humaima Malik along with Imran Abbas Naqvi and Fawad Khan playing the male leads. He is one of the highest paid actors in the industry and is the recipient of several awards including a Filmfare Award, 3 Lux Style Awards, and 6 Hum Awards.
