The "Yu-Gi-Oh!" Trading Card Game, known as the "Yu-Gi-Oh!" Official Card Game (遊☆戯☆王オフィシャルカードゲーム , Yū-Gi-Ō Ofisharu Kādo Gēmu ) in Asia, is a Japanese collectible card game developed and published by Konami.
