Miffy's Adventures Big and Small (Dutch: "Nijntjes avonturen groot en klein" ) is a Dutch-British CGI-animated television series, based on the "Miffy" book series by Dutch artist Dick Bruna. It is a continuation of the "Miffy and Friends" series, which aired from 2003 until 2007. The show airs on KRO in the Netherlands.Miffy and Friends is a Dutch-American stop-motion animated television series, based on the "Miffy" book series by Dutch artist Dick Bruna.
