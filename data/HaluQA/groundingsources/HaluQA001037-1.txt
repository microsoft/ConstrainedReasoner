Catfish is a 2010 American documentary film directed by Henry Joost and Ariel Schulman, involving a young man, Nev, being filmed by his brother and friend, co-directors Ariel and Henry, as he builds a romantic relationship with a young woman on the social networking website Facebook.Yaniv "Nev" Schulman (born September 26, 1984) is an American producer, actor, photographer, and TV host.
