Comic Court (1945–1973) was a most versatile post-war Australian bred Thoroughbred racehorse who set race records at distances of 6 furlongs (1,200 metres) and 2 miles (3,200 metres). He won the 1950 Melbourne Cup carrying 9 st and set an Australasian record of 3 minutes 19½ seconds.The Melbourne Cup is Australia's most prestigious annual Thoroughbred horse race.
