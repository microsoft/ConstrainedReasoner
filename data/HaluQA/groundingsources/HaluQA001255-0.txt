Professor Matthew Bailes is an astrophysicist at the Centre for Astrophysics and Supercomputing, Swinburne University of Technology and the Director of OzGrav, the ARC Centre of Excellence for Gravitational Wave Discovery.Swinburne University of Technology (often simply called Swinburne) is an Australian public university based in Melbourne, Victoria.
