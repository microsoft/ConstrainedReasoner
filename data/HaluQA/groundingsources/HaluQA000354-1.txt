Kim Roberts is a Canadian actress best known for her roles as Christine in "The Handmaids Tale" (2017), Regina from "Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs" (2017), Marnie in "Schitt's Creek" (2015), Neeva in "The Strain" (2014), Gloria in "Lucky Seven" (2013), Camilla in "Being Erica" (2010), Mazz in "The Doodlebops" (2005-06), Mrs. Arvin in "I'm Not There" (2007), Deborah in "Saw III" (2006) and "Saw IV" (2007) and Mrs. Bosco in "The Cheetah Girls" (2003).The Strain is an American horror drama television series that premiered on FX on July 13, 2014.
