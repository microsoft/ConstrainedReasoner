"Bad Girls" is a song by British recording artist M.I.A. for her fourth studio album, "Matangi" (2013). A shorter version of the song appeared on "Vicki Leekx" (2010), a free online mixtape on 31 December 2010.Mathangi "Maya" Arulpragasam (born 18 July 1975), better known by her stage name M.I.A., is a British rapper, singer-songwriter, record producer, and activist.
