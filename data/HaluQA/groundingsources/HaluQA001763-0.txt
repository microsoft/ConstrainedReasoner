Daredevil: The Album was released on March 4, 2003 by Wind-up Records for the movie "Daredevil", starring Ben Affleck.Daredevil is a 2003 American superhero film written and directed by Mark Steven Johnson. Based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name, the film stars Ben Affleck as Matt Murdock, a blind lawyer who fights for justice in the courtroom and on the streets of New York as the masked vigilante Daredevil.
