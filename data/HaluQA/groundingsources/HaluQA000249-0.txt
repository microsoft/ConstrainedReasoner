Aarón Galindo Rubio (born 8 May 1982 in Mexico City, Mexico) is a Mexican football center back, who is playing for C.D. Toledo in Spain.Club Deportivo Toledo, S.A.D. is a Spanish football team based in Toledo, in the autonomous community of Castile-La Mancha. Founded in 1928 it plays in Segunda División B – Group 1, holding home games at "Estadio Salto del Caballo", with a seating capacity of 5,300 spectators.
