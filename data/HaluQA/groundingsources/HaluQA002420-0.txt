The Sikorsky S-1 was the first fixed wing aircraft design by Igor Sikorsky. Further attempts were less successful and Sikorsky disassembled it saving the main wing section to construct the S-2.Igor Ivanovich Sikorsky (Russian: И́горь Ива́нович Сико́рский ; ] , "Ígor' Ivánovič Sikórskij"; , "Ihor Ivanovych Sikorskyi"; May 25, 1889 – October 26, 1972), was a Russian-American aviation pioneer in both helicopters and fixed-wing aircraft. First success came with the S-2, the second aircraft of his design and construction.
