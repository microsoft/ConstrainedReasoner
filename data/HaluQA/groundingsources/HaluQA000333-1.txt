Betzy Alexandra Kjelsberg (1 November 1866 – 3 October 1950), born "Betzy Aleksandra Børresen", was a Norwegian politician for the Liberal Party, being the first female board member of the party, Norway's first female factory inspector from 1910–1936, and a member of the feminist movement. Kjelsberg was born in Svelvik, Vestfold.Vestfold ] is a county in Norway, bordering Buskerud and Telemark.
