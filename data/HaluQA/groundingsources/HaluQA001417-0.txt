Bigheads is a British television game show produced by Primal Media that has aired on ITV since 23 April 2017 and is hosted by Jason Manford with Jenny Powell and Kriss Akabusi as commentators.Kriss Akabusi MBE (born Kezie Uchechukwu Duru Akabusi, 28 November 1958) is a retired British sprint and hurdling track and field athlete.
