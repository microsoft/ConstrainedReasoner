COM LAG (2plus2isfive) is the sixth compilation EP by the English rock band Radiohead, released in March 2004 in Japan and Australia. It is collects many of the B-sides from the singles from their 2003 album "Hail to the Thief", along with some live tracks and remixes by Cristian Vogel and Four Tet. It was released on 9 June 2003, by Parlophone Records in the United Kingdom, and a day later by Capitol Records in the United States.
