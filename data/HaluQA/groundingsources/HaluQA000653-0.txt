 She is perhaps best known for her roles as receptionist Carol Kester on the 1970s sitcom "The Bob Newhart Show", and as the voice of elementary school teacher Edna Krabappel on the animated series "The Simpsons", for which she won an Emmy in 1992. She is the teacher of Bart Simpson's 4th grade class at Springfield Elementary School, and Ned Flanders's wife in later seasons.
