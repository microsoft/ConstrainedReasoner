The South Korean national ice hockey team (Korean: 대한민국 아이스하키 국가대표팀 ; "Daehanmingug Aiseuhaki Guggadaepyotim") is the national men's ice hockey of the Republic of Korea (South Korea).The 2018 IIHF World Championship will be hosted by Copenhagen and Herning, Denmark, as the IIHF announced on 23 May 2014. South Korea will make its debut at the World Championship, having played in the lower divisions previously.
