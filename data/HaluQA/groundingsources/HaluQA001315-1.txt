Steven Ernest "Steve" Sailer (born December 20, 1958) is an American journalist, blogger, and movie critic, a "Taki's Magazine" and VDARE.com columnist, and a former correspondent for UPI.Taki's Magazine, called "Takimag" for short, is an online magazine of politics and culture published by the Greek paleoconservative journalist and socialite Taki Theodoracopulos and edited by his daughter Mandolyna Theodoracopulos.
