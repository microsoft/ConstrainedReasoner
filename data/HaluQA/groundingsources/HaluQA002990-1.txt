João Paulo II Airport (IATA: PDL, ICAO: LPPD) , named after Pope John Paul II, is an international airport located on the island of São Miguel, in the Portuguese archipelago of the Azores. The airport is the major hub for the SATA Group of airlines, which includes both inter-island SATA Air Açores and international Azores Airlines, and since April 2015 as a base for Ryanair. In 2016, Ryanair was the largest European airline by scheduled passengers flown, and carried more international passengers than any other airline.
