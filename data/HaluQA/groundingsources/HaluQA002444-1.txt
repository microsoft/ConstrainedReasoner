HeroClix is a collectible miniatures game that uses the Clix system that centers on the world of superhero comic books, especially the Marvel and DC Comics universes. Players construct teams of comic book heroes, villains, or characters from various video games series such as Street Fighter, Gears of War, and Halo and engage in a turn-by-turn battle on grid maps based on various storyline locations.Street Fighter (ストリートファイター , Sutorīto Faitā ) , commonly abbreviated as "SF" or スト ("Suto"), is a fighting video game franchise developed and published by Capcom.
