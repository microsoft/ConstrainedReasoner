Spliced is a Canadian animated television series produced by Teletoon and Nelvana. The series has aired in Canada on Teletoon, in the United States on Qubo, in Australia on ABC3, in the United Kingdom on Nicktoons, in Latin America on Disney XD, and in Sweden on Nickelodeon.Nicktoons is a British/Irish digital television channel, launched on 22 July 2002.
