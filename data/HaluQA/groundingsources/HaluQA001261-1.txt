 It was released on August 25, 2017, alongside promotional single "Hungry" as the fourth single from Fergie's second studio album, "Double Dutchess" (2017), the same day the album was made available for pre-order. Her debut solo album "The Dutchess" (2006) was a major commercial success on the charts and spawned three "Billboard" Hot 100 number one singles; "London Bridge", "Glamorous", and "Big Girls Don't Cry".
