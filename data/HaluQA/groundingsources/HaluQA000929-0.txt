Ishi: The Last of His Tribe (1978) is a made-for-television biopic based on a book by Theodora Kroeber which relates the experiences of her husband Alfred L. Kroeber who made friends with Ishi, thought to be the last of his people, the Yahi tribe. Kroeber provided detailed information about Ishi, the last surviving member of the Yahi people, whom he studied over a period of years.
