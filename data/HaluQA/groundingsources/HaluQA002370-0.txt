"Feel It Still" is a song by American rock band Portugal. The song reached number one on the US Alternative Songs and Russian Tophit airplay chart, and became their first entry on the Hot 100, peaking at number 10 as of October 2017.Tophit is a Russian music project that deals with online distribution of new songs and music videos to radios and TV-channels respectively as well as publication of music charts, based on the quantity of radio air spins. All charts of Tophit.ru are refreshed every Wednesday.
