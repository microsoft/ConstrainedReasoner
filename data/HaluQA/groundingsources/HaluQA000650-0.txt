 She co-starred in films "Code of Silence" (1985), "Some Kind of Wonderful" (1987), "The Dentist" (1996) and "Election" (1999), and also known for her roles in television on "Herman's Head" (1991–1994) and "Unfabulous" (2004–2007). The show, which debuted in fall 2004, was one of the most-watched programs in the United States among children between the age of 10 and 16 and was created by Sue Rose, who previously created the animated series "Pepper Ann" and "Angela Anaconda".
