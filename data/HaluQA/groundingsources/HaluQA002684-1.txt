Julia Alexandrovna Kourotchkina "(Russian: Юлия Александровна Курочкина, or Yuliya Aleksandrovna Kurochkina "; born 10 August 1974 in Shcherbinka, Russia) is a Russian actress, model and beauty queen who was crowned Miss Russia 1992 and later crowned Miss World 1992 in Sun City, South Africa.Miss Russia (Russian: Мисс Россия) is a national Beauty pageant in Russia.
