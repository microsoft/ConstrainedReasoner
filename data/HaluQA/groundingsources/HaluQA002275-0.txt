Nich Yaka Misyachna (Ukrainian: Ніч яка місячна ) is a Ukrainian song composed by Mykola Lysenko with lyrics from a poem by Mykhailo Starytsky.Evgeny Mikhailovich Belyaev, also written as Yevgeny Belyayev (Russian: Евгений Михайлович Беля́ев) (Klintsy, 11 September 1926 – 21/22 February 1994), was a Russian tenor soloist of the Alexandrov Ensemble under Boris Alexandrov.
