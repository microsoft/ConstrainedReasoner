Tricia Dickson (born January 3, 1982) is an American voice actress. For a brief time, she was also a cast member of the Nickelodeon sketch comedy series, "All That" (where she replaced Katrina Johnson and was later fired and replaced by Danny Tamberelli, and Leon Frierson in 1997).Daniel Paul "Danny" Tamberelli (born February 8, 1982) is an American actor, comedian and musician.
