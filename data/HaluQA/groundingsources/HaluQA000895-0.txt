 Formerly Highway 24W, Highway 42 (its route number a reversal of "24") runs northwest from where Highway 24 crosses the BNSF Railway's Northern Transcon, staying parallel to the tracks until it turns right in Glasgow's downtown and dives under the railroad to meet with and terminate at U.S. Route 2.The Northern Transcon, a route operated by the BNSF Railway, traverses the most northerly route of any railroad in the western United States.
