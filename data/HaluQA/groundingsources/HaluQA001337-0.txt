Katherine Bailess (born April 24, 1980) is an American actress, singer, and dancer best known for playing the role of Erica Marsh on the CW's hit show "One Tree Hill", Life and Death Brigade member Stephanie on "Gilmore Girls", and Kyle Hart on the VH1 series "Hit the Floor".Hit the Floor, originally titled Bounce, is an American sports drama television series on VH1 series, created by James LaRosa.
