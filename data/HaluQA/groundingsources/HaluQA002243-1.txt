Lubabalo Nicholas Kondlo (born December 21, 1971) is a grandmaster of English draughts (also known as American checkers) from South Africa. In 2012 he won the silver medal, the gold being won by Alex Moiseyev, in American checkers at the SportAccord World Mind Games in Beijing, China.IMSA inaugurated the SportAccord World Mind Games December 2011 in Beijing.
