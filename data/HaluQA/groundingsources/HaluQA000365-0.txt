Duane Swank (born May 28, 1953, Decatur, IL) is a scholar of comparative political economy and the welfare state. He received his B.A. from Millikin University and his Ph.D. at Northwestern University.Northwestern University (NU) is a private research university based in Evanston, Illinois, with other campuses located in Chicago and Doha, Qatar, and academic programs and facilities in Washington, D.C., and San Francisco, California.
