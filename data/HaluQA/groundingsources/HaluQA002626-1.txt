In Love and War (1987) is a Vietnam war-based thriller/drama television film starring James Woods and Jane Alexander. The film is based on the true story of James Stockdale and Sybil Stockdale.James Bond Stockdale (December 23, 1923July 5, 2005) was a United States Navy vice admiral and aviator awarded the Medal of Honor in the Vietnam War, during which he was an American prisoner of war for over seven years.
