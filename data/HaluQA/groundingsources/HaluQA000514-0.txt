Pro Wrestling Fujiwara Group ("Purofesshonaru-resuringu Fujiwara-Gumi", プロフェッショナルレスリング藤原組) was a shoot style professional wrestling promotion based in Japan, operating from 1991 to 1996. It was formed by Yoshiaki Fujiwara, Masakatsu Funaki and Minoru Suzuki after the collapse of the Japanese UWF.Minoru Suzuki (鈴木 実 , Suzuki Minoru , ring name: 鈴木 みのる) (born June 17, 1968) is a Japanese professional wrestler and mixed martial artist who is currently working for New Japan Pro Wrestling (NJPW) as a freelancer. He is the current NEVER Openweight Champion in his first reign.
