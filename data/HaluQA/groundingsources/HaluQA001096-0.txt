Kim Hyang-gi (born August 9, 2000) is a South Korean actress. Kim began her career as a child actress, and has starred in films and television series such as "Wedding Dress" (2010), "The Queen's Classroom" (2013), "Thread of Lies" (2014) and "Snowy Road" (2017).Snowy Road () is a 2015 South Korean historical drama film directed by Lee Na-jeong
