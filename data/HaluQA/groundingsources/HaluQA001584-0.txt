Samsung Scholarship was established in 2002 by Samsung to provide financial support, encouragement and opportunities to gifted Korean students pursuing higher education overseas, who have shown the capacity to become global leaders. It was established as Samsung Lee Kun-hee Scholarship, named after the current chairman of Samsung Group who succeeded Lee Byung-chul, the founder.Lee Kun-hee (Hangul:  ; Hanja:  ; ] ; born January 9, 1942) is a South Korean business magnate and the chairman of Samsung Group.
