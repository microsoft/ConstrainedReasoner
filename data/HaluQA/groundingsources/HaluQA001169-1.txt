Roman Lazarevich Karmen (Russian: Рома́н Ла́заревич Карме́н ; 29 November [O.S. 16 November] 1906 in Odessa – 28 April 1978 in Moscow) was a Soviet war camera-man and film director and one of the most influential figures in documentary film making.Francis Stokes is an American screenwriter and film director. Stokes is writer and director of the feature film "Harold Buttleman, Daredevil Stuntman" and the internet video serial "God, Inc.".
