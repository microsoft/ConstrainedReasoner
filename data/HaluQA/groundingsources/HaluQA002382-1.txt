Broken Bridges is a 2006 film starring Toby Keith, Lindsey Haun, Burt Reynolds and Kelly Preston.Kelly Preston (born October 13, 1962) is an American actress and former model. She has appeared in more than sixty television and film productions, most notably including "Mischief", "Twins" and "Jerry Maguire". She is married to John Travolta, with whom she collaborated on the fantasy film "Battlefield Earth".
