Slam Creepers were a rock band from Vansbro, Sweden formed by Björn Skifs in 1962 and broke up in 1969. Skifs would later find success with a number 1 hit "Hooked on a Feeling as a member of Blue Swede.“Hooked on a Feeling” is a 1968 pop song written by Mark James and originally performed by B. J. Thomas. The Blue Swede version made singer Björn Skifs' "Ooga-Chaka-Ooga-Ooga" intro well known (and famous in Sweden at the time), although it had been used originally by British musician Jonathan King in his 1971 version of the song.
