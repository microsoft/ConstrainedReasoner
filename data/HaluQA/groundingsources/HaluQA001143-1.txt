The Menu (), is a 2015 television series produced by Hong Kong Television Network. The series is starred by Noel Leung, Catherine Chau, Kate Yeung and Gregory Wong, written by Pun Man-hung and directed by Ben Fong.Noel Leung Siu-bing (born 7 December 1969 in Macau) is a Hong Kong actress known for her roles in TVB television.
