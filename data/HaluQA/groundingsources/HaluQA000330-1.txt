"Stricken" is a song by the American heavy metal band Disturbed. "Stricken" was used as official theme for WWE's PPV New Year's Revolution, in 2006.New Year's Revolution (2006) was the second annual New Year's Revolution pay-per-view (PPV) event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It took place on January 8, 2006, at the Pepsi Arena in Albany, New York and starred talent from the Raw brand.
