J. Paul Boehmer (born October 30, 1965) is an American actor best known for his numerous appearances in the "Star Trek" universe. His other appearances include "The Thomas Crown Affair" and more recently "The Good German".The Thomas Crown Affair is a 1999 American heist film directed by John McTiernan. The film, starring Pierce Brosnan, Rene Russo and Denis Leary, is a remake of the 1968 film of the same name.
