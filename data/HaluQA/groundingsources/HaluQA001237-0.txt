Mom is a 2017 Indian thriller film directed by Ravi Udyawar. Akshaye Khanna and Nawazuddin Siddiqui play supporting roles in the film.Akshaye Khanna (born 28 March 1975) is an Indian film actor. Since then, he has featured in several critically and commercially acclaimed films, such as "Border" (1997), "Taal" (1999), "Dil Chahta Hai" (2001), "Humraaz" (2002), "Hulchul" (2004), "Gandhi, My Father" (2007) and "Race" (2008) amongst others, for which he has received two Filmfare Awards.
