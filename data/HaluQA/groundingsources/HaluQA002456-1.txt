"Zenzenzense" (Japanese: 前前前世 , "Past Past Past Life") is a song by Japanese rock band Radwimps. It was used as one of the four theme songs to the animated film "Your Name", and was one of 26 songs the band composed for the film's soundtrack.Your Name (Japanese: 君の名は。 , Hepburn: Kimi no Na wa. ) is a 2016 Japanese animated drama film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai and produced by CoMix Wave Films.
