Ann M. O'Leary (born 1971 or 1972) is an American political advisor, lawyer, and nonprofit leader who specializes in early childhood education. She worked in the Bill Clinton administration and for Hillary Clinton during her time as First Lady, Senate career, and 2008 and 2016 presidential campaigns.Hillary Rodham Clinton served as a United States Senator from New York from January 3, 2001 to January 21, 2009. Clinton resigned from the Senate on January 21, 2009 to become United States Secretary of State for the Obama Administration.
