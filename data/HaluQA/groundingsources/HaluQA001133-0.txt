"Episode 8", also known as "May the Giant Be with You", is the first episode of the second season of the American mystery television series "Twin Peaks".Don Sinclair Davis, PhD (August 4, 1942 – June 29, 2008) was an American character actor best-known for playing General Hammond in the television series "Stargate SG-1" (1997–2007), and earlier for playing Major Garland Briggs on the television series "Twin Peaks" (1990–1991).
