Gene Shay (born Ivan Shaner, March 4, 1935 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania) is an American radio personality. He is a representative of Philadelphia's folk music scene. A founder of the annual Philadelphia Folk Festival and its emcee since its inception, he has been called the "The dean of American folk DJs" by "The Philadelphia Daily News" and "The Grandfather of Philadelphia Folk Music" by "The Philadelphia Inquirer". Shay also serves as a host for the online "Folk Alley" stream originating at Kent State University station WKSU and carried on WXPN's website.Kent State University (KSU) is a large, primarily residential, public research university in Kent, Ohio, United States.
