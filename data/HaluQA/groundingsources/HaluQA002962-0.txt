Lee Jong-suk (Hangul: 이종석, born 14 September 1989) is a South Korean actor and model. Lee's breakout role was in "School 2013" (2012) and he is also well known for his roles in "I Can Hear Your Voice" (2013), "Doctor Stranger" (2014), "Pinocchio" (2014) and "W" (2016).W () is a 2016 South Korean television series, starring Lee Jong-suk and Han Hyo-joo.
