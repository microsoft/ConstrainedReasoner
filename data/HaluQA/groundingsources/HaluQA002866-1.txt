"Rattled by the Rush" is a single/EP released by indie rock group Pavement in 1995. It is recognized as the single for the song "Rattled by the Rush", the first single from the band's third album, "Wowee Zowee" (1995).Pavement was an American indie rock band that formed in Stockton, California in 1989. In 2010, they undertook a well-received reunion tour.
