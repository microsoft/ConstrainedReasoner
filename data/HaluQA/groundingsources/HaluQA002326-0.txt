Brenda VanLengen is the primary analyst for the Big 12 Conference women's basketball game of the week on Fox Sports 1 and the San Antonio Silver Stars on Fox Sports Southwest. She also acts as an analyst for BTN, CBS Sports Network, ESPN, FSN, Westwood One, and the Jayhawks TV Network.The Kansas Jayhawks women's basketball team represents the University of Kansas and competes in the Big 12 Conference of NCAA Division I.
