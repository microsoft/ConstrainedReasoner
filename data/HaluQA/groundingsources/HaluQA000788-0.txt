Cody Martin Linley (born November 20, 1989) is an American actor and singer. He played a recurring role as Jake Ryan in the television series "Hannah Montana", and was a contestant on the seventh season of "Dancing With The Stars", in which he was partnered with Julianne Hough and finished fourth.Season seven of "Dancing with the Stars" premiered on September 22, 2008 as a part of ABC's fall 2008 line-up.
