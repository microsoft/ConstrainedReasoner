Sugar is the second studio album by German DJ and record producer Robin Schulz, it was released on 25 September 2015. The album includes the singles "Headlights", "Sugar", "Show Me Love", and "Heatwave"."Sugar" is a song by German DJ and record producer Robin Schulz. The song was released in Germany on 17 July 2015, and samples Baby Bash's 2003 single "Suga Suga".
