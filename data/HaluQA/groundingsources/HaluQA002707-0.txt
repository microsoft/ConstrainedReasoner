Abdelghani Bousta (18 February 1949 in Marrakesh, Morocco – 21 September 1998, buried at Père-Lachaise cemetery in Paris, France) was a Moroccan politician who opposed the monarchical power of his country.Marrakesh ( or ; Arabic: مراكش‎ ‎ "Murrākuš"; Berber: "Meṛṛakec"), also known by the French spelling Marrakech, is a major city of the Kingdom of Morocco. It is the fourth largest city in the country, after Casablanca, Fez and Tangier.
