Michael Edward Curry (born August 22, 1968) is an American retired professional basketball player, and current coach of the Florida Atlantic Owls men's basketball team. He later served as head coach of the Detroit Pistons.The Detroit Pistons are an American professional basketball team based in Detroit, Michigan. Since moving to Detroit in 1957, the Pistons have won three NBA championships in 1989, 1990 and 2004.
