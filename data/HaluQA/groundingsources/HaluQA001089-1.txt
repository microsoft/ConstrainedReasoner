Jesse Lon Plemons (born April 2, 1988) is an American actor. He is also known for his film roles in such films as "Like Mike" (2002), "Observe and Report" (2009), "Battleship" (2012), "The Master" (2012), and as mobster Kevin Weeks in "Black Mass" (2015).Battleship is a 2012 American military science fiction action film loosely based on the board game of the same name.
