Air Vice Marshal Christopher James "Chris" Luck, is a senior Royal Air Force officer. He was Commandant of the Royal Air Force College Cranwell from 2013 to 2016.The Royal Air Force College (RAFC) is the Royal Air Force training and education academy which provides initial training to all RAF personnel who are preparing to be commissioned officers. Originally established as a naval aviation training centre during World War I, the College was established as the world's first air academy in 1919.
