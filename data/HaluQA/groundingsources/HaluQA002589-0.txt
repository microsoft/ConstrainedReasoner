Reynolds Channel, named after William Reynolds, is a strait in Nassau County, New York which separates the Long Beach Barrier Island, that contains the City of Long Beach, and the villages of Atlantic Beach and Lido Beach, from Long Island and Barnum Island.The Atlantic Beach Bridge is a 153 ft long toll drawbridge carrying NY 878, connecting Lawrence and Atlantic Beach (Park Street), New York, while passing over the west end of Reynolds Channel. The bridge also provides direct access to the Rockaway Peninsula via Seagirt Boulevard.
