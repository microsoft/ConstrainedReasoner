 Ala Moana is consistently ranked among the top ten most successful malls in the United States and, in 2009, was ranked by "U.S. News & World Report" as America's second most profitable, behind The Forum Shops at Caesars in Las Vegas, Nevada.The Forum Shops at Caesars (also known as simply The Forum) is a major 636000 sqft shopping mall connected to Caesars Palace on the Las Vegas Strip in Las Vegas, Nevada.
