Steve Young is a television writer for the "Late Show with David Letterman" and "Late Night with David Letterman". He is a Harvard University graduate and former writer for the Harvard Lampoon. He also wrote "The Simpsons" season eight episode "Hurricane Neddy"."Hurricane Neddy" is the eighth episode of "The Simpsons"<nowiki>'</nowiki> eighth season. It was written by Steve Young, directed by Bob Anderson, and features a cameo by Jon Lovitz as Jay Sherman from "The Critic".
