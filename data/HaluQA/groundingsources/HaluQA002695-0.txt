Brigitte Kuhlmann (1947–1976) was a founding member of the West German left-wing militant group "Revolutionäre Zellen" (RZ, or Revolutionary Cells in English; frequently considered a terrorist group). She was killed by the Israel Defense Forces in Entebbe, Uganda, during Operation Entebbe.Operation Entebbe was a successful counter-terrorist hostage-rescue mission carried out by commandos of the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) at Entebbe Airport in Uganda on 4 July 1976.
