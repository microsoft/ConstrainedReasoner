Nostalgia is an upcoming American drama film directed by Mark Pellington and written by Pellington and Alex Ross Perry. It stars Jon Hamm, Nick Offerman, Amber Tamblyn, Patton Oswalt, Catherine Keener, Ellen Burstyn, Bruce Dern, John Ortiz, and James LeGros.Patton Peter Oswalt (born January 27, 1969) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, voice actor and writer, known for roles such as Spencer Olchin in the sitcom "The King of Queens" (1998–2007), voicing Remy in the film "Ratatouille" (2007), playing multiple identical brothers, the Koenigs, on "Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D." (2014–17), and male Jesse in both seasons of Minecraft Story Mode.
