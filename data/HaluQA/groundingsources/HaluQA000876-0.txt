Vladimir Yaroshenko (Russian: Владимир Ярошенко; born November 2, 1985 in Slavyansk-na-Kubani) – Polish-Russian ballet dancer, first soloist with Yury Grigorovich’s Ballet Theatre, Krasnodar, trained in classical Russian ballet school.Yury Nikolayevich Grigorovich (Russian: Ю́рий Никола́евич Григоро́вич ; born 2 January 1927 in Leningrad) is a Soviet and Russian dancer and choreographer who dominated the Russian ballet for 30 years.
