Anatole Litvak (Russian: Анато́ль Литва́к ; May 21, 1902 – December 15, 1974) was a Russian-born American filmmaker who wrote, directed, and produced films in various countries and languages.Kinji Fukasaku (深作 欣二 , Fukasaku Kinji , 3 July 1930 – 12 January 2003) was a Japanese film director and screenwriter.
