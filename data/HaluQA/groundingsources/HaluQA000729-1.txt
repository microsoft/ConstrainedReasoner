Lorton station is a railroad station located at 8990 Lorton Station Boulevard in Lorton, Virginia. Amtrak's "Northeast Regional", "Silver Meteor", "Silver Star", "Palmetto", and "Carolinian" trains share the line but do not stop at either Lorton station.The Northeast Regional is a higher-speed rail service operated by Amtrak in the Northeastern and Mid-Atlantic United States. It is the busiest Amtrak route, carrying 8.04 million passengers in fiscal year (FY) 2013, a 0.4% increase over the 8.01 million passengers in FY2012.
