Kurt Leon Sutter (born May 5, 1960) is an American screenwriter, director, producer, and actor. He worked as a producer, writer, and director on "The Shield," and appeared on the show as hitman Margos Dezerian. Sutter's wife, actress Katey Sagal, was one of the show's lead actresses.Catherine Louise Sagal (born January 19, 1954) is an American actress and singer-songwriter.
