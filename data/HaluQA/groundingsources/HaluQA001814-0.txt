This is a list of adult fiction books that topped "The New York Times" Fiction Best Seller list in 2017, in the Combined Print & E-Book Fiction category. So far, the most frequent weekly best seller of the year is "Camino Island" by John Grisham with 5 weeks at the top of the list, followed by "The Shack" by William P. Young with 4 weeks.The Shack is a novel by Canadian author William P. Young that was published in 2007.
