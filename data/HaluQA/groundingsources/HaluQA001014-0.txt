"Home" is a song recorded by British singer Leah McFall, the runner-up on the second series of the BBC talent show "The Voice", featuring guest vocals from her show mentor will.i.am. This song also featured in the latest film, "The Book of Life".The Book of Life is a 2014 American 3D computer-animated musical fantasy adventure comedy film produced by Reel FX Creative Studios and distributed by 20th Century Fox.
